About

Shawn Kweon is a current Northeastern University PhD student in Rehabilitation Sciences at Bouve College of Health Science. Shawn received his Doctorate of Physical Therapy from Northeastern University in 2024. His clinical and research interests are in the effects of manual therapy on college athletes and injury prevention through wearable devices.
